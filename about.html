Our Journey in Kashmir

Crafting unforgettable memories in the heart of Paradise.

The Roy Legacy

Established with a passion for Kashmir's untouched beauty, Roy Tour & Travels has been the preferred choice for thousands of travelers seeking authentic experiences.

From the snowy peaks of Gulmarg to the tranquil Dal Lake, our deep-rooted local knowledge allows us to craft itineraries that go beyond the ordinary.

We don't just provide tours; we provide entry into the Kashmiri way of life, ensuring every moment is steeped in hospitality and wonder.

Our Vision

To be the leading curator of premium Kashmiri experiences, setting the standard for sustainable and authentic tourism in the valley.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between travelers and the soul of Kashmir through personalized service, local expertise, and unwavering dedication.

Our Values

Integrity, hospitality, and a commitment to preserving the pristine beauty of our homeland guide every decision we make.

Our Pillars of Excellence

What makes Roy Tour & Travels the most trusted name in Kashmiri hospitality.

Tailored Luxury

We redefine luxury by providing bespoke experiences that reflect your personal tastes, from private shikara dinners to high-altitude luxury stays.

Local Roots

Born and raised in the valley, our team has access to hidden meadows and exclusive cultural experiences that regular tour operators simply don't know exist.

Sustainable Path

We believe in preserving the "Aesthetics of Kashmir". Our operations are designed to support local artisans and protect the delicate Himalayan ecosystem.

24/7 Concierge

Your peace of mind is our priority. From the moment you land to your last sunset in the valley, our concierge team is always a phone call away.
