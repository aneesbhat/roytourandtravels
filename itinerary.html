Srinagar · Gulmarg · Pahalgam · Sonamarg

Day-wise Itinerary

Your complete Kashmir journey, planned to perfection — every moment crafted for wonder.

Day1

Arrival in Srinagar — Welcome to Paradise

Touch down at Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport and be greeted by your dedicated Roy Tour & Travels representative. Transfer to your premium hotel or a luxurious houseboat on Dal Lake — your home for the next two nights. After freshening up, enjoy a magical 1-hour Shikara ride gliding through the serene Dal Lake, past floating gardens, lotus blooms and the iconic houseboats. As dusk settles, savour a welcome dinner with traditional Wazwan flavours.

✈ Airport Pickup 🛶 Shikara Ride 🌅 Dal Lake 🍽 Welcome Dinner
Day2

Srinagar City Tour — Mughal Gardens & Old City

Begin your day with a sunrise view of the Himalayas from the houseboat deck. Post breakfast, embark on a full-day Srinagar sightseeing tour visiting the iconic Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), and the fragrant Chashme Shahi. Explore the old Srinagar bazaars at Lal Chowk, visit Hazratbal Shrine and the historic Jamia Masjid. Wind down with a leisurely walk along the Boulevard road as the sun sets over Dal Lake.

🌸 Mughal Gardens 🕌 Hazratbal 🛍 Local Bazaars 🌄 Nishat Bagh
Day3

Gulmarg — The Meadow of Flowers

After a wholesome breakfast, depart for Gulmarg (56 km, ~2 hrs), a high-altitude meadow at 2,730m. Upon arrival, board the world-famous Gondola Cable Car (Phase 1) to Kongdori — one of Asia's highest and longest cable car rides, offering breathtaking panoramas of the Himalayas and Nanga Parbat. Enjoy snow activities (seasonal) including skiing, snowboarding and pony rides across the flower-dotted meadows. Return to Srinagar by evening. Overnight stay.

🚡 Gondola Ride ⛷ Snow Activities 🏔 2,730m Altitude 🌿 Alpine Meadows
Day4

Pahalgam — The Valley of Shepherds

After breakfast, drive to the enchanting Pahalgam (97 km, ~3 hrs) — the basecamp for Amarnath Yatra and one of Kashmir's most stunning valleys. Visit Betaab Valley (named after the Bollywood film), explore Aru Valley with its open meadows and pristine streams, and see the meeting of rivers at Chandanwari. Enjoy a pony ride along the Lidder River banks, surrounded by pine forests reflecting in crystal-clear waters. Overnight at Pahalgam.

🐎 Pony Ride 🌊 Lidder River 🌲 Betaab Valley 🏕 Aru Valley
Day5

Sonamarg — The Golden Meadow

After checking out from Pahalgam, drive to the majestic Sonamarg (125 km, ~4 hrs from Pahalgam). Sonamarg, meaning 'Meadow of Gold', sits at an altitude of 2,800m with glaciers and alpine lakes. Hire a pony or take a local taxi to visit the stunning Thajiwas Glacier — a year-round snow glacier just 2 km from town. Explore the banks of the Sindh River and enjoy the breathtaking valley views before heading back to Srinagar for overnight stay.

🏔 Thajiwas Glacier 🌾 Golden Meadow 🏞 Sindh River ❄ Year-round Snow
Day6

Srinagar — Leisure, Shopping & Crafts

A relaxed day dedicated to immersing yourself in Kashmiri culture and craftsmanship. Visit the famous Suffering Moses and local artisan workshops to see hand-woven Pashmina shawls, Papier-mâché crafts, Kashmiri carpets, and saffron farms. Explore the lively Polo View Market for dry fruits, spices and souvenirs. In the evening, take a sunset Shikara cruise or simply relax on the houseboat deck with Kahwa (Kashmiri green tea) and storytelling.

🧣 Pashmina Shopping 🎨 Artisan Crafts 🛍 Polo View Market 🍵 Kahwa Tea
Day7

Doodhpathri — The Valley of Milk

Today's hidden gem excursion takes you to Doodhpathri (42 km, ~1.5 hrs from Srinagar), a less-explored paradise where milky-white streams flow through vast green meadows — earning it the name "Valley of Milk". The meadows at 2,730m altitude are surrounded by dense forests of pine and fir, with the Himalayas as a backdrop. Enjoy a packed lunch picnic in the meadows, witness wildflowers, and take in the absolute serenity that few tourists experience. Return to Srinagar for a farewell dinner.

🥛 Valley of Milk 🌼 Wildflowers 🌲 Pine Forests 🍱 Picnic Lunch
Day8

Departure — Until We Meet Again

Your last morning in paradise. After a leisurely breakfast, take a final stroll along the Dal Lake boulevard or enjoy one last Shikara ride to bid farewell to the majestic Himalayas. Check out from the hotel, and transfer to Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport for your onward journey. Carry home a heart full of golden memories, the scent of saffron, and the warmth of Kashmiri hospitality — until your next journey with Roy Tour & Travels!

✈ Airport Drop 🏠 Check-out 📸 Last Memories 💛 Farewell
