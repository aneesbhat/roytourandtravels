Terms & Conditions

Guidelines for a smooth and memorable Kashmiri journey.

Booking & Payments

By booking with us, you agree to the following financial terms:

  • Initial deposit: 25% of the total package cost is required to secure hotels and permits.
  • Balance payment: Must be cleared at least 10 days before arrival.
  • Payments can be made via Bank Transfer, UPI, or Credit/Debit cards (merchant fees apply).

Itinerary & Changes

Kashmir's terrain and weather can be unpredictable. Please note:

  • We reserve the right to modify routes in case of road closures, landslides, or security advisories.
  • Changes made by the traveler during the trip may incur additional charges for transport and accommodation.
  • Adventure activities like skiing or rafting depend strictly on weather conditions.

Traveler Responsibilities

Your cooperation ensures a safe and enjoyable journey:

  • Travelers must be medically fit for high-altitude treks/stays (e.g., Gulmarg Phase 2).
  • Original ID proofs must be carried at all times for security check-posts.
  • Respect for local customs, dress codes, and photography restrictions is mandatory.

Disclaimers & Disputes

General legal terms for our services:

  • Roy Tour & Travels is not responsible for loss of personal belongings.
  • Claims for any deficiencies must be reported in writing within 48 hours of service delivery.
  • All legal disputes are exclusively subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Srinagar (J&K).
