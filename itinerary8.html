Day-wise Itinerary

Your complete Ladakh journey, planned to perfection — every moment crafted for wonder.

Day1

Leh Arrival & Sightseeing ✈️

Embrace the spirit of Ladakh as you arrive – welcome! After arriving at Leh airport, you will be transferred to your pre-booked hotel in Leh. Check in and rest for a while before embarking on a sightseeing tour of Leh. You will be visiting Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa, a peaceful Buddhist white domed stupa, and later continue your day tour with a visit to the vibrant Leh market. Afterwards, you'll head back to the hotel for an overnight stay. (Note: Vehicle will be provided as per group size.)

Airport Pickup Sightseeing Dinner
Day2

Sham Valley Trip & Exploring Ladakhi Culture 🏞️

In the morning, you will be driven to Sham Valley. Begin your exploration at the Spituk Gompa and then the Sangam Point, a beautiful confluence of the Zanskar & Indus river. Next, make a stop at the Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, & drive through the mysterious Magnetic Hills. You will also visit the SECMOL School, before heading towards the famous Hall of Fame. Afterwards, you can enjoy a White Water Rafting before returning to your hotel in Leh for an overnight stay.

Breakfast Sightseeing Dinner
Day3

Day Trip Across Khardung-La Pass | Driving Across the Highest all season Motorable Pass ❄️

After breakfast, you will embark on an exciting journey towards the world's highest all season motorable pass - Khardung La Pass. Enjoy the dramatic shift in scenery amidst the mighty Himalayan peak. During your excursion, make a stop at the famous Rinchen cafe for a steaming bowl of Maggi and a refreshing chai. Later, head back to Leh for overnight stay.

Breakfast Sightseeing Dinner
Day4

Trip to Pangong Lake | En-route sightseeing from Pangong Lake Tso to Leh 🏔️

Begin your day trip to the breathtaking Pangong Tso, driving past the rugged terrains of Ladakh. After you arrive at the Pangong Tso, spend the time admiring the dreamy landscapes and the vibrant shades of the lake, changing from blue to turquoise. As you return to Leh via Chang La Pass, you will make stops at the Chang La Baba Temple, Thiksey Monastery and Druk Padma School. Afterwards, you will return to your hotel in Leh for a comfortable overnight stay.

Breakfast Sightseeing Dinner
Day5

Leh Airport ✈️

After breakfast transfer to Leh Airport to connect your onwards destination with great memories of Joy tour and travels.

Breakfast Drop At Ladakh Airport
